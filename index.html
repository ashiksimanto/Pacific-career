WHAT IS RPL? WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

Recognition of Prior Learning gives credit for competencies gained through previous learning, other training, work or life experience as part of skill assessment. It formally recognizes the skills and knowledge that a person already possesses against competencies in the National Training and Vocational Qualifications Framework (NTVQF).



  • 1. Get a nationally recognised Australian qualification within few weeks
  • 2. No class,study,exam or assignment
  • 3. Get qualification based on experience
  • 4. Save time,money & effort

Introduced by the Australian Government, the RPL process awards certificates to people who have related skills and experience.This is a nationally recognised qualification.

STEPS OF RPL COURSES

THE PLANS
Step 1

FREE SKILL ASSESSMENT


Consult with RPL leaders free about your goals who will help you understanding what you need to do and choosing a right qualification for you.

Step 2

EVIDENCE SUBMISSION


Provide evidences that demonstrate your skills which may include CV, reference letter, publication, photos and videos of previous work, certificates and transcripts of overseas qualifications.

Step 3

REVIEW OF SKILL


Your evidence will be reviewed for RPL Australia by reliable RPL experts and an assessment may be conducted through interviews. This will also help you understand your gaps if you have any.

Step 4

BE QUALIFIED


Finally, you will be given a certificate which is valid throughout ​Australia including all​ states and territories. Now, It’s time to discover the benefits of RPL Australia.
